Departments

Explore the diverse and dynamic departments at our engineering college, each offering specialized programs and state-of-the-art facilities.

Computer Science (CS)

Focusing on software development, algorithms, and data structures, preparing students for careers in technology and innovation.

Computer Science AI (CS AI)

Specializing in Artificial Intelligence, this department offers a deep dive into machine learning, neural networks, and intelligent systems.

Computer Science AIML (CS AIML)

Combining AI and Machine Learning, this program is designed to advance the capabilities of intelligent systems and data-driven decision-making.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AIDS)

Focused on AI and Data Science, this department emphasizes data analysis, big data technologies, and predictive modeling.

Information Technology (IT)

Providing in-depth knowledge in IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and software development, essential for the modern tech landscape.

Electronics and Telecommunication (ENTC)

Specializing in communication systems, electronics, and embedded systems, preparing students for the telecom and electronics industries.

Mechanical Engineering

Focusing on design, manufacturing, and thermodynamics, this department is key to careers in automotive, aerospace, and energy sectors.

Chemical Engineering

Focused on chemical processes, materials science, and environmental engineering, essential for the chemical and pharmaceutical industries.
